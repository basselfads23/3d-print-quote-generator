# Privacy Policy — 3D Quote Pro

**Developer:** The Utility Labs
**Contact:** theutilitylabs@gmail.com
**Last updated:** March 26, 2026

---

## 1. Overview

3D Quote Pro is a free Android application developed by The Utility Labs. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information. The short version: **the app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.** Everything you enter stays on your device.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

**We do not collect any personal information.**

The app does not:
- Create user accounts or require registration
- Connect to any server or remote database
- Transmit any data over the internet
- Use analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting services

---

## 3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

All data you enter in the app is stored **only on your device** using your phone's local storage (AsyncStorage). This includes:

- Business profile details (name, email, phone number, description)
- Business logo image (stored locally as encoded image data)
- Materials list (names and prices)
- Print variables (electricity rate, printer wattage, profit margin, wear & tear fee, tax rate)
- Saved client names
- Quote calculation history (retained for 12 hours, then automatically cleared)
- App preferences (currency, weight unit, PDF font)

This data never leaves your device. It is not backed up to any cloud service by the app itself (though your device's own backup systems, such as Google Drive, may back up app data according to your device settings).

You can delete all locally stored data at any time using the **Factory Reset** option in the app's Settings screen.

---

## 4. Photo Library Access

The app requests access to your device's photo library **only** when you choose to add a business logo in Settings. This permission is entirely optional — the app works fully without it.

When you select an image:
- It is stored **locally on your device only**
- It is embedded directly in PDF invoices you generate
- It is never uploaded to any server or shared with any third party by the app

---

## 5. PDF Generation and Sharing

The app can generate PDF invoices from your quote data. These PDFs:
- Are created and stored entirely on your device
- Are shared only when **you** actively choose to share them (via your device's native share sheet — e.g., WhatsApp, email, etc.)
- Are not uploaded, transmitted, or accessible to The Utility Labs or any third party

---

## 6. Third-Party Services

3D Quote Pro does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or data collection services. No data is sent to third parties of any kind.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

This application is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. Since the app collects no data from any user, this applies equally to all age groups.

---

## 8. Data Security

Because all data remains on your device and is never transmitted, there is no server-side security risk. The security of your data depends on the security of your device itself (e.g., screen lock, device encryption). We recommend keeping your device protected with a PIN or password.

---

## 9. Your Rights and Data Deletion

Since no data is collected or stored by us, there is no data held by The Utility Labs to request access to, correct, or delete.

To delete data stored locally on your device:
- Open the app → **Settings** → **Factory Reset App** to erase all app data
- Or uninstall the app, which removes all locally stored data from your device
